Add semantic markup

<html>
  <body>
    <script type="application/ld+json">
    {
      "@context" : "http://schema.org",
      "@type" : "Event",
      "name" : "Google I/O 2013",
      "startDate" : "2013-05-15T08:30"
    }
    </script>
    <p>
      Dear John, thanks for booking your Google I/O ticket with us.
    </p>
    <p>
      BOOKING DETAILS<br/>
      ...  </body>
</html>

Final notes

  • Invalid markup is ignored
  • Test email must be sent from the same account
  • Google Now cards get prioritized by algorithms
  • Google Now cards appear at certain times - watch out for time zones
  • Google Now cards must refer to real events - e.g. flight number must be a real one
